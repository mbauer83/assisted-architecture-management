## Product Owner

A concrete actor (human) who uses the architecture repository for product planning and requirements validation. Navigates the motivation and strategy domains to verify that product requirements are adequately realized in the architecture. Uses discovery tools to trace requirements through capabilities to implementation, identifying gaps and misalignments.

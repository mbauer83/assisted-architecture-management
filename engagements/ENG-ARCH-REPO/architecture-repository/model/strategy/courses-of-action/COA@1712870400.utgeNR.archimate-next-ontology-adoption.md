## ArchiMate NEXT Ontology Adoption

The strategic decision to adopt the ArchiMate NEXT ontology over ArchiMate 3.x. Key motivations: NEXT consolidates behavioral elements (services, processes, functions, events, collaborations, roles) into a shared "common" domain, eliminating the artificial duplication across business/application/technology domains. NEXT renames "layers" to "domains", correctly reflecting that they represent aspects of concern rather than strict abstraction layers. This aligns with the system's goal of supporting both human and automated participants who perform the same behavioral functions regardless of domain classification.

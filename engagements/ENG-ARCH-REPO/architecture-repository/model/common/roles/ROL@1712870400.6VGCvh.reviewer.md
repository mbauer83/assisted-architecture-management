## Reviewer

A role performed by either a human user or an AI agent when validating and reviewing architectural artifacts. Reviewers use the discovery and validation services to assess artifact quality, verify cross-domain coherence, and approve artifacts for status transitions (draft → active) or promotion to the enterprise repository. The verification tools automate much of the mechanical review work, allowing human and AI reviewers to focus on semantic correctness and architectural judgment.

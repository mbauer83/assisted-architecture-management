## Datatype–Data Object Relationship Consistency

A classifier may be bound to a Data Object; a structural relation between two Data-Object-bound classifiers and the ArchiMate relation between their Data Objects must correspond in semantic class and direction — enforced bidirectionally on verification and in authoring, with permitted relation types derived from the configured ontology at runtime, never hardcoded.

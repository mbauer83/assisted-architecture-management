## Tamper-Evident Assurance Records

The system must maintain an append-only, hash-chained audit log and sealable hash-anchored baselines, with optional RFC 3161 timestamping and configurable retention; stronger WORM immutability and crypto-shred are opt-in for regulated tiers.

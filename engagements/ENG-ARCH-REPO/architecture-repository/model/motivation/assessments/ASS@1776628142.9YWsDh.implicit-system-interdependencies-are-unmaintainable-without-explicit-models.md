## Implicit System Interdependencies Are Unmaintainable Without Explicit Models

As software systems grow in component count and team scale, the web of dependencies between components, services, teams, and technology layers becomes impossible to reason about correctly without explicit, navigable architectural models; without such models, changes propagate through undocumented paths causing drift, regression, and integration failures.

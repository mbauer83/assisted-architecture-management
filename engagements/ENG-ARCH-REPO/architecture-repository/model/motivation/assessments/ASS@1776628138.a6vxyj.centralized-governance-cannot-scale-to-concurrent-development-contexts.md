## Centralized Governance Cannot Scale to Concurrent Development Contexts

Traditional architecture review boards and centralized governance mechanisms are structurally unable to scale to the number of concurrent autonomous teams, AI-driven workstreams, and multi-engagement contexts characteristic of modern software organizations, making tool-enforced governance and distributed standards essential.

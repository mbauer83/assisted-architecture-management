## Configurable documents

System should permit freely specifying document-types with a name, type-abbreviation, frontmatter-schema, optionally required sequence of sections (h2-headers, marked by `##`) and two optional lists of required and of suggested entity-type references. The latter feature makes it possible to require e.g. a standards-document to have to link to at least one Requirement-Entity of the model in order to be considered valid and implemented similar restrictions for other document types.

## Metadata-Based Querying

The system shall support querying artifacts by their structured metadata — artifact type, status, keywords, version, last-updated date, and any other frontmatter fields. Queries shall be expressible through the tool interfaces (MCP, CLI, REST) and support filtering, sorting, and aggregation over the SQLite runtime index. This enables precise artifact discovery when the user knows what kind of artifact they need but not its exact identity.

## Unique IDs for Entities, Connections, Diagrams, and Documents

Every artifact in the repository shall have a globally unique identifier. Entity IDs follow the convention `TYPE@epoch.random.friendly-name` encoded in the filename. Connection IDs are reconstructed as `{source}---{target}@@{type}`. Diagram and document IDs follow similar filename-based conventions. Uniqueness is guaranteed by the combination of epoch timestamp and cryptographically random 6-character suffix, making ID collisions negligible even across distributed, concurrent authoring.

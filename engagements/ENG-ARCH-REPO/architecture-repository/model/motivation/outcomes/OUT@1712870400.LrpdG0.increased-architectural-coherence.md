## Increased Architectural Coherence

A measurable outcome of deploying the architecture repository: architectural artifacts across the organization become more consistent, traceable, and internally coherent. Measured by: reduction in referential integrity errors, increase in cross-domain connection coverage, decrease in orphaned entities (entities without connections), and stakeholder-reported improvement in architectural clarity. This outcome is realized when the goals of coherence, traceability, and cross-stakeholder validation are achieved.

## Properties

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Metric | Referential integrity error count, connection coverage ratio, orphaned entity count |
| Target | Zero integrity errors; >80% of entities connected to at least one other entity |

## Support Product Design Evolution and Validation for AI

Enable AI agents to participate in product design evolution by querying requirement-to-realization traces, proposing new connections, and validating design changes against the existing model. AI agents can programmatically traverse the dependency graph to assess the impact of proposed changes, identify unrealized requirements, and suggest architectural refinements that better serve product goals.

## Properties

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Priority | Should |
| Measurability | AI agents can traverse requirement-realization chains and identify unrealized requirements |

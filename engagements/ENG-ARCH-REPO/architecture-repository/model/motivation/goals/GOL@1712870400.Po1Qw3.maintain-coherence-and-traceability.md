## Maintain Coherence and Traceability

Ensure that all architectural artifacts — entities, connections, diagrams, and documents — remain internally consistent and traceable across ArchiMate NEXT domains. Every requirement should trace to a goal, every capability to a realization, every application component to its technology substrate. The repository's verification tools enforce referential integrity, status conformity, and cross-domain connection validity, making incoherence immediately detectable rather than silently accumulating.

## Properties

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Priority | Must |
| Measurability | Zero referential integrity errors in verified model; complete realization chain from motivation to technology |

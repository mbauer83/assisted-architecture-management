## Maintain Coherence and Traceability

Ensure that all architectural artifacts — entities, connections, diagrams, and documents — remain consistent, indexable, searchable and traceable. Every requirement should trace to an outcome or goal, every outcome and goal to stakeholders and drivers and vice versa. Every interface should trance to to a service, every service to one or more processes. As architecture models grow and are edited by a diverse set of (human and AI) authors and editors, we need to maintain this coherence and traceability.

## Properties

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Measurability | Zero referential integrity errors in verified model; complete realization chain from motivation to technology |
| Priority | Must |

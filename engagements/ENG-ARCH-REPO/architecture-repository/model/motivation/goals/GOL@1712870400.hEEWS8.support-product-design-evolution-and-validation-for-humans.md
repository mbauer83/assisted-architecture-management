## Support Product Design Evolution and Validation for Humans

Enable human product owners and designers to track how product requirements evolve through architectural realization — from motivation (goals, requirements) through strategy (capabilities) to implementation (components, interfaces). The repository provides visual cross-domain views and queryable traceability that allow humans to validate that product design intent is faithfully reflected in the architecture, and to identify gaps or misalignments as designs evolve.

## Properties

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Priority | Should |
| Measurability | Product stakeholders can trace requirements through to realization and identify gaps |

## Support Technology Design Evolution and Validation for Humans

Enable human engineers (developers, DevOps, architects) to track how technology decisions evolve and validate that the technology stack realizes application-domain requirements. The technology domain diagrams and cross-domain views show how nodes, system software, and artifacts support application components, allowing engineers to assess infrastructure adequacy, identify technology debt, and plan migrations with full visibility into upstream dependencies.

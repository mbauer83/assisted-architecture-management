## Product Owner

The product owner who uses the architecture repository for product planning, requirements validation, and design evolution tracking. Navigates the motivation domain to verify that goals, requirements, and capabilities align with business intent. Uses cross-domain views to validate that product features trace from business need through to technical realization.

## Properties

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Category | Individual |
| Concerns | Requirements traceability, product-architecture alignment, planning visibility, stakeholder communication |

## Notes

Product owners primarily consume the motivation and strategy domains but benefit from the full cross-domain realization chain that demonstrates how business goals translate into implemented capabilities.

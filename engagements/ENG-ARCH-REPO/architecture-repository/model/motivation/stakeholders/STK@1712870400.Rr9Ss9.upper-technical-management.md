## Upper Technical Management

Senior technical leadership (CTOs, VPs of Engineering, principal engineers) who use the architecture repository for governance oversight, strategic planning, and cross-team coherence. They need high-level views of the capability landscape, the realization chain from goals to technology, and the ability to assess architectural health across the enterprise and its engagements.

## Properties

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Category | Group |
| Concerns | Enterprise-wide coherence, strategic alignment, governance capacity, reuse across teams, architectural health visibility |

## Notes

Upper technical management is the primary beneficiary of the two-tiered repository structure (enterprise + engagement), as it enables governance at scale without requiring centralized control of every engagement.

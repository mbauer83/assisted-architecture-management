## Architect

The enterprise or solution architect who creates, maintains, and evolves architectural models across all ArchiMate NEXT domains. The architect is the primary author of motivation, strategy, and cross-domain views, ensuring coherence between business intent and technical realization. In this system, the architect works through tool-mediated interfaces (CLI, MCP, GUI) to author entities, define connections, and produce diagrams that communicate design decisions to all other stakeholders.

## Properties

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Category | Individual |
| Concerns | Model coherence, cross-domain traceability, ontology correctness, diagram expressiveness, reuse across engagements |

## Notes

The architect may be human or AI-assisted. The architecture repository is designed so that both human architects working through a GUI and AI agents working through MCP/CLI can perform authoring and validation with equivalent capability.

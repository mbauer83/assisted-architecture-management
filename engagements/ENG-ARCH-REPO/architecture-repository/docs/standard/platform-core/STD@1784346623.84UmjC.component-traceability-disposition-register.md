## Scope

The 27 application components a conformance scan on 2026-07-17 found without a forward
structural chain (composition/aggregation/assignment/realization, direct or derived up to
4 modeled hops) to any goal or requirement. Every scan candidate gets exactly one status;
nothing sits unclassified across scans.

## Motivation

Untraced components read as governance gaps whether they are real gaps, legitimate
plumbing, or scan false-positives. A disposition register makes each judgement explicit,
reviewable, and expiring — a waiver is an honest owner decision, not a silent proof.

See requirement [Verified Referential Integrity](../../../projects/motivation-narrative/model/motivation/requirement/REQ@1712870400.Ee3Ff3.verified-referential-integrity-model-diagrams-documents.md)

## Summary

Of the 27 candidates: 5 conform (4 via derived forward chains, 1 repaired with direct
realization edges), 11 were repaired on 2026-07-18 with one realization edge each (each
edge states its real fulfilment claim), and 11 are waived as interface/write-pipeline
plumbing or external systems, governed via their parent's requirements. No candidate was
a scan false-positive. Waivers expire 2027-01-18 and are re-reviewed by the model owner.

### False-positive (0)

None — the scan's forward-derivation rule produced no wrong classifications.

### Review protocol

Re-run the component-traceability-gaps view after each scan; assert the four statuses
still exactly partition the candidate set. Expired waivers are re-reviewed: either the
waiver is renewed with a fresh rationale or the component is repaired.

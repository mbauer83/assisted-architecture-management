## Validate Promotion Selection

Validates that the selected artifacts form an acceptable promotion set under applicable rules and decisions. This includes STRUCTURAL CLOSURE for entities with no standalone meaning: a selected junction requires all of its incident non-junction endpoints (transitively through junction chains) and a selected grouping requires all of its composition/aggregation members — each satisfiable by inclusion in the selection, existing enterprise presence, or a global reference. Violations block the plan with the exact missing entities (no silent auto-widening: promotion stays an explicitly selected, reviewed set) because a partially promoted junction or grouping would not merely be incomplete — the dropped connections ARE its meaning, and they would be erased rather than left behind.

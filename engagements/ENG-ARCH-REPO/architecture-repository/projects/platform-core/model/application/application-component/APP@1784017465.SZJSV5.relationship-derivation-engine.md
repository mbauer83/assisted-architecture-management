## Relationship Derivation Engine

Computes indirect (derived) relationships from chains of real modeled connections, following the ArchiMate Appendix-B derivation rules (certain DR and potential PDR composition, role/strength ordering, the direct-model boundary, and the deliberate exclusion of association from further composition). Runs a bounded, memoized traversal at query time — nothing derived is persisted. Shared by viewpoint execution (derived-traversal neighbor inclusions, connection selection, and relationship.hops derived attributes), the graph-neighbors read tool, and derived-diagram generation. A wall-clock time budget stops an unbounded search gracefully, returning a partial result flagged as truncated with a warning rather than an error.

## Specialization Profile

The conceptual pairing of a concept specialization (entity or connection) with the attribute profile it authors against — pick a specialization, get its attribute schema. A specialization contributes attributes three ways: inline on its own declaration, a dedicated attachment schema, and bindings to named, reusable profiles that several specializations may share. The specialization's own contribution always wins last over any shared profile it composes. A specialization can exist without any attributes (pure taxonomy/notation); a binding to a profile no registry defines is a structural failure surfaced at startup.

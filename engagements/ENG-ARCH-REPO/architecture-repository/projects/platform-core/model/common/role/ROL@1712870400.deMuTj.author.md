## Author

A role performed by either a human user or an AI agent when creating or modifying architectural artifacts. Authors interact with the authoring service through one of the tool interfaces (GUI for humans, MCP/CLI for AI agents) and are responsible for providing the semantic content of artifacts — descriptions, property values, connection rationale — while the tooling handles structural concerns (frontmatter, IDs, index updates).

| Performed By | Architect, Developer, Product Owner (human); AI coding/planning agents |

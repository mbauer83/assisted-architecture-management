## Low-Friction Adoption

The standing worth, to a prospective platform adopter, of being able to trust the platform enough to commit before investing specialist effort: honest documentation, a working first-run path, and guidance that carries method knowledge they do not yet have. Kept interest-relative deliberately — any measurable adoption target would be an outcome, not this value.

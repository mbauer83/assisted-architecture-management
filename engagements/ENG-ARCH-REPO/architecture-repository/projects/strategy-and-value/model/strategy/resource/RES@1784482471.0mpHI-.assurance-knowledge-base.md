## Assurance Knowledge Base

The accumulated confidential assurance knowledge — hazard analyses, control structures, risks, obligations, assurance cases, and supply-chain signals — held in the classification-gated assurance store and bound to the architecture model. A strategic asset because assurance arguments compound across releases and audits; losing it would mean re-deriving safety and compliance rationale from scratch.

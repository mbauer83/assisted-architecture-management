## Architecture Knowledge Management

The ability to capture, organize, and verify architecture knowledge as a coherent, queryable model. This is what the product fundamentally is, however it is built: the ability survives a complete re-implementation with different components and processes (enterprise at rest). Distinct from the service "Architecture Management System" (the consumer-facing view at an interface) and from the authoring functions (behavior performed by concrete assignable components of this product's operating model): those realize this ability, they are not it. Distinct pairwise from "Architecture Analysis & Visualization": this ability keeps the knowledge correct and organized; that one derives answers and presentations from it.

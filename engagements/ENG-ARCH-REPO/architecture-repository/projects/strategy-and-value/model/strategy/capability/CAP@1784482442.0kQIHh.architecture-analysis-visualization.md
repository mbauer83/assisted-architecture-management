## Architecture Analysis & Visualization

The ability to derive answers and presentations from architecture knowledge: traversals, coverage and impact analysis, viewpoint projections, and diagrams. Pairwise distinct from "Architecture Knowledge Management": that ability keeps the knowledge correct and organized; this one turns it into insight for decisions and communication. The ability survives re-implementation with different query engines, renderers, or presentation surfaces.

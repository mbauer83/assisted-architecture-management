## Tiered Knowledge Governance

The standing ability to govern architecture knowledge across trust tiers: engagement-local working knowledge and a reviewed, reusable enterprise baseline, with controlled movement between them. Named for the ability at rest — promotion is the motion that exercises it, so the name deliberately avoids "promotion" (the process "Promote Artifacts" and its functions realize this ability; they are the operating model, this is the business model). The ability would survive re-implementing the tiers on entirely different storage or review machinery.

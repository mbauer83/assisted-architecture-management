## Integrated Safety, Security & Compliance Assurance

The ability to perform safety, security, and compliance assurance work (hazard and threat analysis, risk treatment, compliance tracking, assurance cases, supply-chain risk) integrated with the architecture model rather than in disconnected specialist tooling. The ability is stable across re-implementation: it does not depend on the current STPA/CAST/GRC module structure or store technology. It anchors the strategy trace for supply-chain security signals: external dependency risk becomes architecture-bound assurance knowledge.

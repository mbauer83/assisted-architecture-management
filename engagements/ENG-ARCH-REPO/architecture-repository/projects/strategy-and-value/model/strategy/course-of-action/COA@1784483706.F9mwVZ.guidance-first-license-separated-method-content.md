## Guidance-First, License-Separated Method Content

The chosen approach of carrying modeling method knowledge as importable, license-separated guidance content rather than baking it into the software. Revocable (one could hardcode the method texts); the separation is what makes an open-source release legally clean while keeping method depth available at authoring time. Realizes the deployment-level guidance-import obligation and aligns with the extensibility-and-configurability principle.

## Increasing Demand for More Autonomy of Teams & Systems

Modern organizational design favors greater autonomy for teams and subsystems — accepting higher failure rates in exchange for increased innovation velocity and iteration speed (cf. contingency theory). However, organizations that achieve integration (shared understanding of goals, values, and requirements; reusable learnings) even through increased diversification will outperform those that cannot.

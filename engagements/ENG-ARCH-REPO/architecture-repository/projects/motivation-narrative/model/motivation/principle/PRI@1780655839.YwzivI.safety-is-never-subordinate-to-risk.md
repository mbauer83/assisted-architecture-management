## Safety Is Never Subordinate to Risk

Safety constraints are absolute and cannot be overridden by cost, schedule, or risk-acceptance decisions; the system enforces this with a hard safety-disposition safeguard on every assurance write.

## Properties

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Priority | Must |
| Rationale | Safety constraints must remain absolute to prevent any cost, schedule, or risk-tolerance tradeoff from weakening them; the system enforces this invariant architecturally via the safety-disposition safeguard on every assurance write |

## Support Models, Diagrams, and Documents

The system shall support three primary artifact categories: architectural models (entities and their connections organized by ArchiMate 4.0 domains), diagrams (PlantUML visualizations referencing model entities), and documents (architectural decision records, guidelines, specification documents with structured cross-references). All three categories share the same frontmatter schema, ID convention, and verification infrastructure, providing a unified framework for all forms of architectural knowledge.

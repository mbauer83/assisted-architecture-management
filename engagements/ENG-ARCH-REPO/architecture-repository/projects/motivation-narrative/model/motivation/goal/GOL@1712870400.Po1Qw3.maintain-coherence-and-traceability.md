## Maintain Coherence and Traceability

Ensure that all architectural artifacts — entities, connections, diagrams, and documents — remain consistent, indexable, searchable and traceable. Every requirement should trace to an outcome or goal, every outcome and goal to stakeholders and drivers and vice versa. Every interface should trace to a service, every service to one or more processes. As architecture models grow and are edited by a diverse set of (human and AI) authors and editors, we need to maintain this coherence and traceability. A coherent, traceable model yields the signals to validate that what is planned and built serves the stated goals, and to locate conflicts between goals, requirements and designs early — a value that holds independently of how code production evolves.

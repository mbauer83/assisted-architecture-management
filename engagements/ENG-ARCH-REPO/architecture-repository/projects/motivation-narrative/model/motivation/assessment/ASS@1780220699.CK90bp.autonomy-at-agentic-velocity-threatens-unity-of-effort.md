## Autonomy at Agentic Velocity Threatens Unity of Effort

As autonomous, AI-driven SDLC work raises velocity and the number of concurrent contexts, the integration needed for unity of effort — shared understanding of plans, governance and what is built — erodes unless deliberately and efficiently maintained. The erosion is at the integration between contexts: structuring work locally, through per-team specifications and conventions, does not by itself restore shared understanding of plans, governance and what is built across teams, agents and engagements.
